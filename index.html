About

The purpose of this calculator is to help you make informed purchasing decisions with your internet plans. Internet Providers love to sell you things to don't need. And a very high percentage of their customers do not need 102 Megabits per second. Try out the calculator, we tried to make it as full of options as we could to help you make a more informed decision.

Important Make sure you do simultaneous usage. You probably do several of these activities through the day, but when measuring bandwidth it is about simultaneous usage.

We believe that the cable industry, especially in the United States, is convincing their customers that they need more speed. We're trying to help you save money.

We weclome suggestions and improvements, our site is up on Github and we will take pull requests for improvments and fixes.

Disclaimer

We are striving for accuracy on our calculator, but the totals you get may not reflect how much bandwidth you need or use in a given month. All values are our best estimates of usage.
